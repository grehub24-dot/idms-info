How to Pay School Fees

Payment can be made at any branch of the following approved banks:

  • GCB Bank
  • Ecobank
  • Zenith Bank
  • Consolidated Bank Ghana (CBG)
Instruction: Mention "AAMUSTED School Fees" and provide your Student Index Number and Full Name to the teller.

Mobile Money (Shortcode)

Dial *887*18# on all networks and follow the prompts. Select "AAMUSTED" from the list.
